# Privacy Policy for Smart NID Helper

**Effective Date:** July 11, 2026

Thank you for using the Smart NID Helper extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection & Storage
The Smart NID Helper extension does not collect, transmit, or share your personal data with any external servers. All data extracted from your National ID (NID) or manually entered in the companion web application is stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's local storage mechanism (`chrome.storage.local`).

## 2. Usage of Data
The data stored locally is used strictly for the purpose of automatically filling the official DoNIDCR enrollment form. The extension only injects this data when you explicitly click the "Auto-Fill" button on the official government portal.

## 3. Data Deletion
You maintain full control over your data. You can delete saved profiles directly from the extension's popup interface at any time. Uninstalling the extension will also clear all locally saved data.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The extension interacts with the official DoNIDCR portal (`enrollment.donidcr.gov.np`). The extension is not affiliated with the Government of Nepal. Please refer to the official government portal for their own privacy practices regarding the data you submit to them.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our official website: [https://aadarshapandit.com.np](https://aadarshapandit.com.np).
